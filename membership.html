Membership

Membership is ONLY for the Radiologists.

Membership Categories:

Primary membership:

1. Radiologists – MD / DMRD/ DNB degree in Radiology / Radio-diagnosis [MCI accredited].

  • A. Founder Member: Members who joined FMRC at its inception [6th April-30th April 2024].
  • B. Life Member: Members who joined FMRC after paying life membership fee.
  • C. Honorary Member: A person considered suitable and desirable by the Executive Committee may be enrolled as a Honorary member [National or International].
  • D. Overseas Member: Medical practitioner registered outside India and practicing Fetal MRI shall be eligible for membership of this category.

2. Radiology Post-graduate student - Students pursuing the MD / DMRD/ DNB degree course in Radiology / Radio-diagnosis.

Primary membership Fee:

  • 1A. Founder Member: Rs 5000/- [Life membership].
  • 1B. Life Member: Rs 7000/- [Life membership].
  • 1C. Honorary Member: Complimentary.
  • 1D. Overseas Member: Rs 10,000/- [Life membership].
  • 2. Radiology Post-graduate student : Provisional Membership [with limited privileges and no voting rights] : Rs. 7000/- This provisional membership will be converted to Life Membership after the student submit their degree certificates [without need for any additional payment].

Membership Application process:

The online membership form should be filled and the degree certificates and MCI registration certificates should be submitted by email.

The application and the documents will be reviewed by the FMRC office. The selected applicants will be contacted over the phone and email and the payment details will be shared directly accordingly.
